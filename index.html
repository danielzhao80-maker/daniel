How to Play

1. Enter Your Name

Type your name on the home screen. This saves your progress, titles, and leaderboard scores.

2. Roll for a Class

Click New Game then Roll to get a random class. Costs 100 🍖 rotten flesh per roll. Higher rarity = stronger class!

Rarities: Common (40%) → Rare (30%) → Epic (18%) → Legendary (10%) → Mythical (2%)

3. Controls

WASD or Arrows — Move
Mouse — Aim
Click — Shoot / Attack
R — Reload
E — Special Ability
1 2 3 — Speed Runner modes
ESC or P — Pause
Right Click — Admin Teleport (if enabled)

4. Fight Monstrosities!

Each wave, a monster type is rolled (Frost, Flame, Shield, Mad Mom, or Miku). Kill all monsters to complete the wave. Monsters get stronger each wave!

5. Rotten Flesh 🍖

Monsters drop 10 🍖 each. Use flesh to roll for classes. Pick up ❤️ health, 🔫 ammo, and 🍖 flesh drops from killed monsters.

6. Points 🏆

Earn 1 point every 20 waves (world clear). Use points to:
• Level up characters (up to Lv.3) for stat boosts
• Select a specific class for your next roll
• Convert: 100 🍖 = 10 🏆 or 1 🏆 = 10 🍖

7. Worlds

🏙️ City — Start here
🏜️ Desert — Clear 20 waves to unlock
🎪 Circus — Clear 40 waves to unlock
Each world has different colors and vibes!

8. Codes

Enter codes in the code pad on the home screen for free rewards! Each code works 10 times per day.

9. Auto-Save

Game saves every 5 seconds and after each wave. Close the browser and come back — hit Continue to pick up where you left off!
